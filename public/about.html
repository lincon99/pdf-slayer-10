About PDF Slayer

PDF Slayer is a free, browser-based OCR tool that allows users to extract text from PDFs and images instantly. All processing happens locally in your browser, ensuring complete privacy and speed.

Features:

  • ✅ Extract text from PDFs and images (JPG, PNG, BMP)
  • ✅ Supports English & Hindi
  • ✅ Fast, secure, 100% free
  • ✅ No sign-up required

Why Choose PDF Slayer?

Our mission is to make document digitization simple and accessible for everyone. We prioritize security, privacy, and performance, and continuously improve our OCR engine for better accuracy.
