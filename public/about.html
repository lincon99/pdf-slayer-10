About PDF Slayer

PDF Slayer is a free browser-based OCR tool that extracts editable text from PDFs and images instantly. It supports English and Hindi, works locally in your browser, and ensures complete privacy.

Our Mission

We aim to make document digitization simple, secure, and accessible to everyone — students, professionals, and researchers alike.

Why Choose PDF Slayer?

  • ✅ Fast local processing (no uploads)
  • ✅ Works on PDFs and common image formats
  • ✅ Supports multiple languages
  • ✅ 100% free and no registration required
  • ✅ Designed with privacy-first principles
