About PDF Slayer

PDF Slayer is a fast and secure Optical Character Recognition (OCR) tool that extracts text from scanned PDFs and images directly in your browser. It supports both English and Hindi languages with high accuracy. No upload to external servers — all processing happens locally, ensuring your privacy and data safety.

How It Works

Simply upload your PDF or image file below, click "Extract Text", and let the built-in AI OCR engine do the rest. A red progress bar shows live progress as your file is processed, and the extracted text appears instantly below.

Supported Formats

PDF, JPG, JPEG, PNG, and BMP formats are supported. You can copy, edit, or save your extracted text freely for reports, assignments, or documentation work.
